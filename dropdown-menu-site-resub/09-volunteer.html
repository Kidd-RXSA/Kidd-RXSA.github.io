Become a Volunteer

Make a difference in the lives of our furry and mythical friends by joining our volunteer program.

Why Volunteer?

Volunteers play a critical role in helping us care for our animals, maintain our facilities, and spread awareness about adoption. Whether you have a few hours a week or a few days a month, your time can help make a world of difference.

How You Can Help

  • Assist with animal care and feeding
  • Help with adoption events
  • Clean and maintain animal habitats
  • Spread the word about our shelter

Ready to get started? Contact us today to learn more about our volunteer opportunities and how you can join our team!
